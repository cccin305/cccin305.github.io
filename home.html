Ace at Home

Try to spend at least one third of your time at home and keep it in good order. Keeping a good home not only saves you time and money, it also makes a good impression on guests if they pay you an unexpected visit!

To be an Ace at House:

  1. Maintain your house in a neat and tidy condition all times
  2. Keep a few simple things in your house that you really enjoy and get rid of the rest
  3. Pay your bills on time

It's time to work!

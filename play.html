Ace at Play

We all like to play, and while playtime is important, rememeber, it must be balanced with work and home - so don't ever let playtime cut into work or home time becasue this will lead to danger and very bad habbits.

To be an Ace at Play:

  1. Use playtime to learn new skills
  2. Know when its time to stop playing at the end of the day and in life
  3. Play with people who are better at it than you are

It's time to go home.
